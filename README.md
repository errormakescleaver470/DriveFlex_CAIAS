# DriveFlex_CAIAS
DriveFlex is an on-demand driving learning platform that connects learners with nearby instructors for flexible, slot-based lessons. Users can book sessions, track progress, and pay online, while drivers earn part-time by teaching in their local area.
DriveFlex Academy is an innovative online platform designed to simplify and modernize the process of learning how to drive. It connects learners with certified driving instructors, allowing users to book lessons anytime and anywhere.
The platform focuses on three core pillars: Safety, Confidence, and Progress, ensuring that learners receive high-quality, reliable training tailored to their needs.
